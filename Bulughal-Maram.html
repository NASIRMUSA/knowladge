A classic compilation of hadith pertaining to Islamic jurisprudence, meticulously organized by the great scholar Ibn Hajar. Each hadith is accompanied by its chain of narration and the scholars who authenticated it.

About This Book

Author

Ibn Hajar al-Asqalani (1372-1449 CE) was a medieval Shafiite scholar and historian who authored numerous works including the monumental commentary on Sahih al-Bukhari.

Key Features

  • Contains 1,371 hadith
  • Organized by fiqh chapters
  • Includes authenticity grading
  • References to major hadith collections

Description

Bulugh al-Maram is one of the most important hadith collections focusing on Islamic jurisprudence. Ibn Hajar selected authentic hadith from various sources including Sahihain (Bukhari and Muslim) and organized them according to legal topics. The book is widely studied in Islamic universities and seminaries worldwide.
