A classic collection of hadith from the Prophet Muhammad (SAW) focusing on Islamic morals, manners, and worship. Compiled by Imam Nawawi, this timeless work remains essential for every Muslim household.

Book Details

Author Information

Imam Nawawi (631-676 AH) was a renowned Syrian scholar and jurist. He authored numerous Islamic works and was known for his piety and knowledge.

Publication Details

  • Language: Arabic (with translations available)
  • First Published: 13th century
  • Chapters: 372 hadith chapters
  • Category: Hadith Collection

Description

Riyadus Saliheen is one of the most popular compilations of hadith, containing authentic sayings of the Prophet Muhammad (SAW) organized by topics of Islamic jurisprudence. It covers all aspects of life, from worship to social conduct.
